You always wanted to work on a specific topic, but didn't yet find time? That's the occasion. **When?** We come together the *last Thursday of the month*, so on the same day we also have our usergroup meetup. We start as of *9 am*. **Where?** [localhost Leipzig GbR], Sternwartenstraße 31, 04103 Leipzig [[Map]] [localhost Leipzig GbR]: http://localhost-leipzig.de/ [Map]: https://goo.gl/maps/zuyPo **What?** Free choice of topics! The idea is to both work commonly on a topic as also to watch what others are doing or to support each other. Amongst PHP we'll cover the web and open source in general, servers and Linux, databases, IDEs, editors and tools like git or zsh. This month we'll work on [KirbyCMS] from our last [usergroup meetup] and [Fulltext RSS] from FiveFilters. Further topics will be [vtigerCRM], [XmlSplitter] and IRC Hacks.
